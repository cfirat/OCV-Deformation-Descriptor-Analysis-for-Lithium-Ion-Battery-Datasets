## CALCE A123 Dataset
CALCE Battery Data, Center for Advanced Life Cycle Engineering, University of Maryland.
URL: https://calce.umd.edu/battery-data

Raw data are not redistributed here. The repository contains processed descriptor tables generated from the public datasets.
